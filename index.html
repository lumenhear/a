About Me

A Clinician Who Truly Listens

I believe every voice deserves to be heard — and every child deserves the chance to find theirs.

I am a BASLP (Bachelor of Audiology & Speech-Language Pathology) trained clinician with extensive experience in speech and hearing rehabilitation across Chandigarh and surrounding regions. My practice is grounded in the latest evidence-based protocols, delivered with warmth and genuine patient focus.

From diagnosing hearing loss in newborns to helping stroke survivors rediscover their words, I offer comprehensive audiological and speech-language services tailored for every stage of life.
